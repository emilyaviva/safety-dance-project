About the Safety Dance Web Application

Everyone comes from a different background, but one thing that unites us all is the need for safe bathroom facilities. For many transgender people, entering a gender-specific bathroom can be a source of stress and anxiety, because using the restroom can mean very real health and safety concerns. Harassment of trans people in and around gender-specific bathrooms can range from denial of use to police intervention to verbal threats and physical assault.

In this application we have attempted to create a simple application where users can share information on public restrooms anonymously.
